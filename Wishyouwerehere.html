Wish You Were Here

Made in 1975

Wish you were here was composed by David Gilmore, and features a tribute song to Syd Barret who went insane from drugs.

Fun Fact The album cover of a man on fire shaking another mans hand had acutely been lit on fire.

This album is the follow up to Dark Side Of The Moon and was on top charts for 10 years.
