Pink Floyd Dark Side Of The Moon

Made in 1973

Pink Floyds first hit written by Roger Walter and David Gilmore after Syd Barret left the band after going crazy.



Pink Floyd choose the prism as the album cover because it depicts multiple colours and stories.
